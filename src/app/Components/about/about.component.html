About us

We are Porters

We are making delivery seamless and hassle-free.

Established in Nigeria on February 2019 as Porter Express Delivery Limited, it was created to make on-demand and same-day delivery possible for everyone at the touch of a button. Today, we operate across the 36 Nigerian states , offer global import and export shipment with our courier and delivery partners across the world.

Our driver and delivery partners operate a vast array of vehicles to suit each market including lorries, vans and cars for deliveries of almost anything of any size , providing fast and low-cost delivery solutions.
